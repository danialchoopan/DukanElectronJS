### Gap 4: Enhanced Inventory Report — Stock Aging (MEDIUM)
From spec §4: "Stock Aging & Turnover Reports — identify slow-moving, obsolete inventory."

**Add a new IPC handler** `products:stockAging` that queries:
```sql
-- Group products by how recently they were last sold
SELECT p.id, p.title, p.stock, p.purchase_price,
  MAX(si.createdAt) as lastSoldAt,
  CAST(julianday('now') - julianday(MAX(si.createdAt)) AS INTEGER) as daysSinceSold
FROM products p
LEFT JOIN sale_items si ON si.productId = p.id
WHERE p.isActive = 1 AND p.stock > 0
GROUP BY p.id
ORDER BY daysSinceSold DESC
```

**Add UI section** in Inventory view (3rd tab "گزارش انبار" / Inventory Report):
- Category-level valuation breakdown (total stock value by category)
- Slow-moving items list (products not sold in 30+ days)
- Top 10 products by stock value
