Accounting System

Double-entry accounting with automatic journal entries, financial reports, and analytics.

1. Chart of Accounts

Uses a hierarchical chart of accounts with self-referencing parentId. Account types: asset, liability, equity, income, expense.

2. Automatic Journal Entries

Every financial transaction automatically posts double-entry journal entries via the repository functions.

Sale (Cash)

Dr. Cash (1100)               += total_amount
Cr. Sales Revenue (4100)      += total_amount
Dr. Sales COGS (5100)         += total COGS
Cr. Inventory (1300)          += total COGS

Sale (Credit/Ledger)

Dr. Accounts Receivable (1400) += total_amount
Cr. Sales Revenue (4100)       += total_amount
+ Customer ledger entry

Purchase

Dr. Inventory (1300)          += total_amount + tax
Cr. Supplier Payable (2110)   += total_amount + tax
+ Supplier ledger entry
+ Stock increment per item

Expense

Dr. Expense Account (6100-6700) += amount
Cr. Cash (1100)                += amount

Return (Sale)

Dr. Sales Revenue (4100)     -= refund amount
Cr. Cash (1100)              -= refund amount
Dr. Inventory (1300)         += restored stock value
Cr. Sales COGS (5100)        += restored COGS

4. Validation Rules

  • Journal entries: total debit must equal total credit (tolerance 0.01)
  • Account deletion: blocked if journal entries reference it
  • Customer/supplier deletion: blocked if balance is non-zero
  • Sale creation: stock must be >= quantity (safety guard in decrementStock)
  • Rounding: totals rounded to nearest 500 tomans (configurable)

5. Analytics Tab

The "Analytics" tab in Accounting provides interactive charts:

  • P&L Bar Chart: Revenue, COGS, OpEx, Net Profit comparison
  • Expense Donut: Operating expenses by account (toggleable bar/donut)
  • Revenue Breakdown: Per-account revenue bars
  • Balance Distribution: Assets vs Liabilities vs Equity progress bars
  • Cash Flow: Inflow/Outflow/Net summary cards
  • Journal Volume: Monthly entry count bar chart

Controls: text size (sm/md/lg), 4 color themes, PNG export, chart type toggles.
