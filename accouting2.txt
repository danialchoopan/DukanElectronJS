1. Chart of Accounts & General Ledger

**Current State:** Basic chart with fixed accounts, no flexibility.

**Required Enhancements:**
- Build a fully customizable Chart of Accounts where users can:
  - Create, edit, and delete accounts (Assets, Liabilities, Equity, Income, Expenses)
  - Assign account codes/numbering (flexible numbering system)
  - Set account types and sub-account hierarchies
  - Mark accounts as active/inactive
- Implement Double-Entry Accounting System:
  - Every transaction must affect at least two accounts (debit/credit)
  - System must enforce debit = credit before saving
  - Provide validation for unbalanced entries
- Fiscal Periods Management:
  - Allow users to define fiscal year start date (e.g., March 21 for Iranian calendar)
  - Create monthly, quarterly, and annual periods
  - Ability to close periods (lock transactions in closed periods)
  - Ability to reopen periods with admin override
- Trial Balance Report:
  - Real-time report showing all account balances
  - Filter by date range and account type
  - Export to Excel and PDF
  - Show debit/credit columns with totals that match

**Priority: CRITICAL (Foundation of the system)**

### 2. Accounts Receivable & Payable Management

**Current State:** No proper tracking of customer/supplier balances.

**Required Enhancements:**
- Customer & Supplier Master Database:
  - Full contact information (name, phone, email, address, tax ID)
  - Opening balance entry
  - Transaction history view
  - Account statement generation
- Invoicing & Bill Tracking:
  - Create sales invoices with line items, tax, discounts
  - Record purchase bills from suppliers
  - Assign due dates and payment terms (e.g., Net 15, Net 30)
  - Invoice numbering: automatic with custom prefix (e.g., INV-2026-0001)
  - Support for Credit Notes and Debit Notes
- Aging Reports:
  - AR Aging: Show what customers owe you by aging buckets (0-30, 31-60, 61-90, 90+ days)
  - AP Aging: Show what you owe suppliers by aging buckets
  - Color-coded for easy identification (green = current, yellow = 30+, red = 60+)
- Partial Payments & Payment Tracking:
  - Accept partial payments on invoices
  - Automatically track remaining balance
  - Link payments to specific invoices
  - Support for multiple payment methods (cash, check, bank transfer, credit card)
- Early Payment Discounts:
  - Define discount terms (e.g., 2% discount if paid within 10 days)
  - Auto-calculate discount on early payment

**Priority: HIGH (Core daily operations)**

### 3. Cash & Bank Management

**Current State:** Basic cash in/out without bank reconciliation.

**Required Enhancements:**
- Multi-Bank Account Support:
  - Add multiple bank accounts (checking, savings, merchant accounts)
  - Starting balance entry for each account
  - Transfer funds between accounts
- Check Management:
  - Register received checks from customers (with check number, bank, date)
  - Register issued checks to suppliers
  - Check status tracking: Pending, Cleared, Bounced, Void
  - Auto-reconcile checks when they clear the bank
- Bank Reconciliation:
  - Upload or manually enter bank statement transactions
  - Match system transactions with bank statement (auto-matching by amount and date)
  - Flag discrepancies for manual review
  - Generate reconciliation reports showing differences
- Petty Cash Management:
  - Create petty cash accounts with assigned custodians
  - Record daily expenses (with receipt attachments - see image upload feature)
  - Replenish petty cash when low
  - Track balance per petty cash account
- Cash Flow Forecasting:
  - Project future cash inflows (based on due invoices)
  - Project future cash outflows (based on due bills)
  - Show projected daily/weekly cash position
  - Alert when cash is projected to fall below threshold

**Priority: HIGH (Essential for financial control)**

### 4. Sales & Purchase Management

**Current State:** Basic invoice creation without proper integration.

**Required Enhancements:**
- Professional Invoice Templates:
  - Sales Invoice (standard format with logo, address, tax info)
  - Purchase Order (for ordering from suppliers)
  - Proforma/Quotation (before sale)
  - Credit Note (for returns/refunds)
  - Debit Note (for additional charges)
  - Customizable templates (users can add logo, customize layout)
- Line Item Management:
  - Add products/services with description, quantity, unit price, discount
  - Support for multiple tax rates (e.g., 0%, 5%, 9%, VAT)
  - Subtotal, tax total, shipping, and grand total
  - Show discount as percentage or fixed amount
- Incidental Costs Handling:
  - Add shipping, insurance, customs, handling fees to purchase invoices
  - Allocate these costs to product cost (affects inventory valuation)
  - Show cost breakdown on purchase details
- Multi-Currency Support:
  - Set base currency (default)
  - Create invoices in foreign currencies
  - Auto-update exchange rates (or manual entry)
  - Show equivalent amount in base currency
  - Track gains/losses from currency fluctuations
- Pricing Tiers:
  - Define price levels: Retail, Wholesale, VIP, Promotional
  - Assign specific prices to customer groups
  - Auto-select appropriate price tier when customer is selected
- Auto-Email Invoices:
  - Send invoices directly from the app via email
  - Use customizable email templates
  - Support for attachments (PDF invoice)
  - Track if invoice was viewed/opened
- Multiple UOM (Units of Measure):
  - Define UOM conversions (e.g., 1 Carton = 12 Pieces)
  - Purchase in one UOM, sell in another
  - Auto-convert on invoices

**Priority: HIGH (Primary business operations)**

### 5. Financial Reporting & Analytics

**Current State:** Very limited reports, mostly manual Excel exports.

**Required Enhancements:**
- Profit & Loss Statement (Income Statement):
  - Show revenues, COGS, gross profit, expenses, net profit
  - Comparative: Current period vs. previous period
  - Filter by date range, cost center, project
  - Percentage of revenue calculations
- Balance Sheet:
  - Assets = Liabilities + Equity structure
  - Current assets, fixed assets, current liabilities, long-term liabilities
  - Show retained earnings
  - Comparative with previous periods
- Cash Flow Statement:
  - Operating activities (cash from customers, paid to suppliers)
  - Investing activities (purchase of assets, sale of assets)
  - Financing activities (loans, equity, dividends)
  - Direct or indirect method (choose one)
- Executive Dashboard:
  - Real-time KPIs: Today's Sales, Month-to-Date Revenue, Outstanding AR, Current Cash Balance
  - Charts: Revenue trend (monthly), Expense breakdown (pie chart), Cash flow (bar chart)
  - Quick links to reports and actions
  - Customizable widgets (users can choose what to show)
- Comparative Reports:
  - Month-over-Month comparison
  - Year-over-Year comparison
  - Budget vs. Actual comparison (if budget module exists)
  - Show variance and variance percentage
- Cost Center / Project Tracking:
  - Assign transactions to departments (Sales, Marketing, R&D, etc.)
  - Track expenses by project/job
  - Generate profit per department or project
  - Allocate indirect costs (overhead allocation)

**Priority: HIGH (Decision-making tool)**

### 6. Advanced Features & Automation

**Current State:** Manual entries for everything, no automation.

**Required Enhancements:**
- Recurring Transactions:
  - Define recurring entries (rent, salaries, insurance, subscriptions)
  - Set frequency: Monthly, Quarterly, Annual, Custom
  - Auto-generate entries on scheduled dates
  - Option to auto-post or require approval
- Fixed Assets Management:
  - Register fixed assets with purchase date, cost, useful life, salvage value
  - Auto-calculate depreciation: Straight-line, Declining Balance, Sum-of-Years
  - Track accumulated depreciation and net book value
  - Generate asset schedule report
  - Disposal/sale of assets with gain/loss tracking
- Multi-Currency Management (Advanced):
  - Real-time exchange rate updates from API (e.g., currencylayer, Fixer.io)
  - Revaluation of foreign currency accounts at period end
  - Realized vs. Unrealized gains/losses
  - Historical rate tracking for audit trail
- VAT / Tax Management:
  - Define tax rates (e.g., 0%, 5%, 9%, 13%)
  - Apply tax per line item or on total
  - Record tax liability (output VAT on sales, input VAT on purchases)
  - Generate VAT return report (summary of tax collected vs. paid)
  - Submit VAT reports in required format (country-specific)
- User Role Permissions (RBAC):
  - Predefined roles: Admin, Accountant, Sales Rep, Warehouse Manager, Viewer
  - Custom roles with granular permissions:
    - View: Read-only access
    - Create: Can add new records
    - Edit: Can modify existing records
    - Delete: Can delete records
    - Approve: Can approve/post transactions
  - Restrict access to reports based on role
- Audit Trail:
  - Log all user actions: Create, Update, Delete, View (sensitive data)
  - Record: Timestamp, User ID, Action, Affected Record, Old Value, New Value
  - Searchable audit log with filters (date, user, action type)
  - Immutable logs (cannot be deleted or modified)
  - Export audit trail for compliance

**Priority: MEDIUM-HIGH (Differentiator features)**
